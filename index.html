El Pollo Loco is a game where you get to live out your wildest chicken-killing fantasies! As a brave warrior armed with nothing but a few bottles to throw, you must fight your way through hordes of evil chickens to take down the dreaded end boss. These chickens are no pushovers - they're tough, fast, and more than a little bit crazy! But don't worry, with your lightning-fast reflexes and killer bottle-throwing skills, you're more than a match for them. Wraps will also increase your health! So get ready to show those chickens who's boss and save the village from their feathered tyranny!
